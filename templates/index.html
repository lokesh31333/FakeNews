Welcome to Fake News Detection Appliation

This system leverages Bidirectional LSTM (BiLSTM), a type of neural network architecture, combined with LIME, a text explainer, to detect and explain fake news. BiLSTM processes text bidirectionally for improved NLP tasks, while LIME provides interpretability by explaining the model's predictions

It is a powerful fusion of deep learning and model explainability for accurate and transparent fake news classification.

Disclaimer: While our app strives to provide accurate analysis in detecting fake news, it is important to understand that no technology can guarantee 100% accuracy. The results offered by this app should not be taken as definitive or used as the sole source for news authenticity. Users are encouraged to cross-reference information and consult additional sources to form a well-rounded understanding of the news.
